What is a Jaap Counter App?

A jaap counter app is a digital tool used to count mantra repetitions (jaap), similar to a traditional mala with 108 beads. It helps track naam jap, mantra chanting, and spiritual routines without losing count.

Unlike a physical mala, a digital mala counter app provides accurate counting, daily tracking, multiple mantra profiles, and complete history of your chanting practice.
