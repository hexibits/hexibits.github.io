Data Retention and Deletion

How long we keep your data:

The EchoForge app stores all your profile data (including Song profile name and profile settings) locally on your device only. We do not store this information on our servers. This data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the application.

For advertising identifiers shared with Google AdMob, Google retains this data according to their privacy policy. You can reset your advertising ID at any time from your device settings.

How to delete your data:

Since all your EchoForge data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

Clear all app data: Go to your device Settings → Apps → EchoForge → Storage → Clear Data.

Uninstall the app: Uninstalling EchoForge will remove all locally stored data from your device.

Request assistance: If you need help deleting your data, contact us at hexibitsapps@gmail.com and we will provide guidance.

Note: We do not have access to your profile information remotely, as this data never leaves your device.
